SECTION 4: VIVA SURVEY STATUS
==============================================================================

The VIVA survey (Chung+2009, AJ, 138, 1741) observed 53 Virgo galaxies in HI.
VizieR catalog J/AJ/138/1741: DOES NOT EXIST on VizieR
VIVA Data Archive: http://www.astro.yale.edu/viva/protected/dataindex.html
  (Could not access - SSL certificate error)

VIVA provides: HI channel maps, total intensity maps, velocity fields,
velocity dispersions, global/radial profiles, position-velocity diagrams.
VIVA does NOT provide extracted rotation curves in the Paper I atlas.
However, the velocity fields and position-velocity diagrams can be used
to derive rotation curves.

ACTION NEEDED: Access VIVA data archive at Yale to download HI cubes/velocity fields.
Then derive rotation curves using 3DBarolo or tilted ring fitting.
